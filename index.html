Our services

Apartment & Rooms

At Flux Hotel, we offer a range of stylish accommodations designed for your ultimate comfort. Whether you’re here for a quick stay or an extended visit, our rooms and apartments provide the perfect blend of modern amenities and thoughtful design, ensuring a relaxing and memorable experience.

Food & Drinks

At Flux Hotel, we take pride in offering a diverse and vibrant culinary experience. Whether you’re in the mood for a gourmet meal, casual bites, or refreshing cocktails, our dining options cater to every palate and occasion.

Fitness & Spa

At Flux Hotel, we believe in the importance of wellness and relaxation during your stay. Our state-of-the-art Fitness Center and luxurious Spa are designed to help you recharge, rejuvenate, and maintain your health routine while away from home.

Event & Party

At Flux Hotel, we specialize in hosting unforgettable events and parties that leave lasting impressions. Whether you're planning a corporate meeting, wedding reception, birthday celebration, or a private party, our versatile spaces and exceptional service ensure that every event is seamlessly executed.

Sports & Games

we understand the importance of staying active and entertained during your stay. That’s why we offer a variety of sports and games facilities to help you unwind, stay fit, and have fun. Whether you're a sports enthusiast or just looking for some casual recreation, we have something for everyone.

Gym & Yoga

we are committed to helping you maintain your wellness routine while you travel. Our fully equipped Gym and serene Yoga studio provide the perfect environment for both intense workouts and mindful relaxation, ensuring you stay fit and balanced throughout your stay.
